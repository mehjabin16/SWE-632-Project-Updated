The Classic Lasagna Recipe

| 4.7 (546)

While there are a few steps to this lasagna recipe, it’s easy to make and has so much flavor. This dish can be made ahead of time and freezes well either before or after baking!

Ingredients

Meat Sauce Preparation

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef and pork
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes and tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil and dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper and salt

Cheese Filling Preparation

  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Directions

Cook noodles

In a well-salted water pot, boil the 9 lasagna noodles and cook for 6-7 minutes . Remove the pasta from the water and lay flat on a tray to cool.

Make the meat sauce

In a large saucepan, cook the onion, celery, and garlic. Add in the ground beef and pork and cook until brown. Next, add oregano, onion powder, sage, salt, and red pepper. Add tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes.

Make cheese mixture

In a bowl, combine cottage cheese, parmesan, ricotta, black pepper, parsley, and eggs.

Layer the lasagna

Add some of the meat sauce to the bottom of the casserole dish. Next, add a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top of the sauce. Spread 1/2 of cottage cheese mixture over noodles followed by more of the meat sauce and shredded mozzarella cheese. Repeat these layers one more time and top off the whole dish with leftover sauce and mozzarella cheese.

Cook and serve

Bake the lasagna for 50 to 60 minutes until it’s hot and bubbly. Let it cool before slicing!
